New paper, “Modelling geospatial distributions of the triatomine vectors of Trypanosoma cruzi in Latin America” is now published in PLOS NTD.

We use so called target-group background approach to construct background points for presence-only data while adjusting for sampling bias and Generalized Additive Models (GAMs) to estimate the relative presence probability for 30 triatomine species.
