In 2013, ZEIT ONLINE and the Statistical Consulting Unit developed a method to calculate coalition probabilities for the German general election.

For the the general election 2017 #BTW17, we additionally provide a shiny app with up to date probabilities based on the most current surveys

   

Updates are also provided via Tweets by KOALA_LMU

   

The R package coalitions contains open source code for our calculations
